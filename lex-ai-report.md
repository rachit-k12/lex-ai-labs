## Company Mission & Vision

### Mission

To build India's most ambitious community of AI practitioners—engineers, founders, and leaders who ship production AI systems and accelerate each other's growth through real connections, not just content.

### Vision

To create a high-trust network where the next generation of AI builders in India learn from those who've shipped at scale, access opportunities before they hit the market, and accelerate their careers through meaningful peer relationships.

### Core Philosophy

"Access people, not just content." Lex AI Labs believes in the power of community-first learning—where practitioners who ship code at scale mentor, collaborate, and guide peers on real-world problems. The community is the hero; the programs are included, but not the main selling point.

## Products & Services

### 1. The AI Fellowship Program (Flagship Offering)

**Overview:** A comprehensive, immersive AI/ML training program designed to transform engineers and professionals into AI practitioners who can build and deploy real-world AI systems.

**Program Structure:**

- **Format:** Live weekend sessions (Saturdays)
- **Duration:** Multi-week intensive program
- **Delivery Mode:** Hybrid (In-person and online options available)
- **Session Timing:** Weekend classes to accommodate working professionals

**Curriculum Journey:**
The fellowship takes participants through a progressive learning path:

1. **Classical Machine Learning Foundations**

   - Fundamental ML concepts and algorithms
   - Supervised and unsupervised learning
   - Data preprocessing and feature engineering
   - Model evaluation and validation

2. **Deep Learning**

   - Neural networks architecture
   - Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
   - Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
   - Advanced DL techniques

3. **Modern AI & Large Language Models**

   - Transformer architecture
   - Prompt engineering
   - Fine-tuning LLMs
   - Building and training custom GPT models

4. **Generative AI & Applications**
   - Generative models and techniques
   - AI agents and agentic systems
   - RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
   - Multi-agent systems

**Key Features:**

- **Live Weekend Sessions:** Interactive, instructor-led classes
- **1:1 Mentorship:** Personalized guidance from industry experts
- **Project Reviews:** Regular feedback on hands-on projects
- **Career Guidance:** Job placement support and career counseling
- **Industry Expert Sessions:** Guest lectures from AI practitioners at leading companies
- **Access to Learning Materials:** Books, videos, and supplementary resources
- **Capstone Projects:** Real-world AI implementation projects
- **Build Your Own GPT:** Hands-on experience training custom language models

**Learning Approach:**

- **70% Practical, 30% Theory:** Emphasis on hands-on implementation
- **Project-First Learning:** Building real AI systems from day one
- **Real-World Problem Solving:** Industry-relevant use cases
- **Portfolio Building:** Creating demonstrable AI projects

**Target Audience:**

- Software engineers looking to transition into AI/ML roles
- Technology professionals wanting to upskill
- Students preparing for AI careers
- Professionals seeking to integrate AI into their current roles
