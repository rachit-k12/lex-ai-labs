===============================================================================
DESIGN SYSTEM SUMMARY
===============================================================================

PRIMARY PALETTE (Most Used Colors):
-----------------------------------
1. neutral-900 (#171717) - Headlines, dark buttons, dark sections
2. neutral-600 (#525252) - Body text, descriptions
3. coral-500 (#FF7F50) - Primary accent, warm highlights
4. white (#ffffff) - Main backgrounds, cards
5. neutral-50 (#fafafa) - Section backgrounds
6. coral-50 (#fffbfa) - Warm subtle backgrounds

SUPPORTING COLORS:
------------------
- neutral-100 to neutral-800 - Various UI elements
- blue-500/600 - Limited accent (headlines, links, hovers)
- green-50/600 - Success/positive outcomes
- purple-50/600 - Event variety
- coral-100 to coral-400 - Warm gradients, decorative elements
- #0077b5 - LinkedIn brand

COLOR STRATEGY:
---------------
1. Warm & Premium - Coral accent creates warmth vs cold blues
2. Editorial Feel - Serif headlines with neutral palette
3. High Contrast - Dark headlines on light backgrounds
4. Subtle Warmth - Coral-tinted backgrounds vs pure white
5. Strategic Color - Color for CTAs, accents, and brand moments
6. Grainy Texture - Adds tactile, premium quality feel

===============================================================================
ACCESSIBILITY & CONTRAST
===============================================================================

PRIMARY TEXT COMBINATIONS (WCAG AA Compliant):
-----------------------------------------------
- neutral-900 on white - Excellent contrast - Headlines
- neutral-600 on white - Good contrast - Body text
- neutral-500 on white - Acceptable - Secondary text
- white on neutral-900 - Excellent contrast - Dark sections
- coral-500 on white - Check for accessibility on small text

CAUTION COMBINATIONS:
---------------------
- neutral-400 on white - Minimum for small text only
- coral-400 on white - Large text only (price highlights)
